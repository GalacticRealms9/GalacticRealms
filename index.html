What is OrbitCraft?

OrbitCraft is a Minecraft server for the Minecraft Java Edition. Orbit Craft has had a long and sought-out journey—let's take a trip through it together. I was introduced to a kid who was six years older than me, and despite the age gap, we shared a common blessing: autism. After some discussion, I decided to join him and help manage his Minecraft Realm. However, as we were expanding, disaster struck. Someone came in and destroyed our world, the one we had worked so hard to build. We were devastated, as this happened frequently not only to us but to others as well. That’s when I offered to embark on a journey together. Now, almost four years later, I’m happy to say that I’m glad I took this path. Orbit Craft is a place where we encourage innovative ideas and provide a safe space for people to unwind after work or school. In my younger days, gaming frustrations led me to break a few phones when I would come home or wake up to find all my hard work destroyed. But here, you can be whoever you want to be. Want to play riskily? Go ahead. Dream of living in outer space and fighting custom bosses? It’s possible. Want to rule as a king in the overworld or underwater? The world is your oyster; you choose. Orbit Craft is more than just a server—it’s a home. We've had players from our earliest days stick with us because they know that when they come home from school or work, they can have fun without losing everything to player or admin abuse. They get to hang out in a cool environment and meet great people who just want to play the game. So, I invite you not only to Orbit Craft but to Galactic Realms. The universe is in your hands. Why settle for less?

Story about OrbitCraft

Orbit Craft is a treacherous yet beautiful story that I’m excited to share with all of you. I had just come home from a near-death experience, and although I wasn’t actively seeking friends, my mom introduced me to someone who shared a common blessing: Gabe. Despite being over five years older than me, he was fascinating—looking like a teddy bear with not a care in the world. Our journey began when he introduced me to his Minecraft Bedrock Realm. I joined eagerly, knowing that my mom would have to get me a PC so I could not only play with him but also explore other games that she wasn’t too fond of (and still isn’t). After becoming what you might call partners, disaster struck. Someone force-op’d themselves, practically hacking the server. Without the ability to back up realms, we lost hours of progress—not just ours but also that of others in the community we were building. That’s when I realized I was paying for a Minecraft server that I wasn’t running. And so, it all began. We first went by FBRD, a Towny/Slimefun server. In essence, it was mods without the hassle of installing them, allowing everyone to play. Soon after, I invited another kind soul, Joel, to join us. This was a huge win, and we managed to dominate the market by offering not just Minecraft but also Ark in the same community. Players could transfer currency from one game to another, allowing for easier or more challenging experiences depending on their preference. Sadly, all good things came to an end sooner than we hoped. Gabe left us to create content on YouTube and provide a safe space there. When I thought that was the end, Joel vanished due to real-life complications and may never return. They were both dearly loved, and their absence was deeply felt. So, do we quit? I’ve endured much more than most people know, but I will never back down because the only person who can be me is me. With that mindset, I took sole financial responsibility and brought Banana on board. He is now the highest-ranking person in our community other than me. To put it simply, I’m the head chairman, and he manages the board of directors. With our combined knowledge, we built what is now known as Orbit Craft. Our goal is to rebuild to our former glory, whether or not our fallen brothers are with us. I invite you to join us and give us a chance. Be a part of building a future for gaming—not only for yourself but for your friends and their friends, and so on. We’re tackling servers full of corruption and driven by profit, and we’re taking a new approach to create a safe haven for all. This is Galactic Realms. We fear no one. The universe is in your hands.
